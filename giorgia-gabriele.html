Table of Biography

Giorgia Gabriele is a famous Italian fashion designer, model, and entrepreneur. Giorgia Gabriele is famous as the founder and creative director of the fashion line “Wandering”. Moreover, she is also famous as the celebrity wife of Andrea Grilli, the director of “Marcelo Burlon”.

Early Life

Giorgia Gabriele was born on December 27, 1985, and she is currently 38 years old. She hails from Sora, Lazio, Frosinone, Italy, and her zodiac sign is Capricorn. She later also lived in Milan and Bologna. There, she pursued her love for fashion.

Moreover, she also has an educational background in Business and Banking Economics. Furthermore, she has not shared any insightful information regarding her parents, siblings, and other family details.

Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Giorgia Gabriele is a high-end fashion designer. She is also a model and entrepreneur. She is also a founder and creative director of a clothing and accessory label called “Wandering”.

She founded this company in the year 2016. With this company, she was able to pursue her passion for fashion. Likewise, she has also dabbled in modeling. The young lady is also an Instagram phenomenon with more than 2 million followers.

Furthermore, she then had her entertainment debut as a showgirl on Italian television. Moreover, an Instagram video of her and Gianluca Vacchi dancing to Ricky Martin’s La Mordidita went viral. In the year 2008, she competed in the Italian television show called “Veline”. It was after a friend suggested her about the show. On the show, she was the winner of episode 57 of the show.

Giorgia Gabriele has certainly gained a huge fan following through her modeling career. As of now, she endorses brands like Hermes, Louis Vuitton, and many more. She has also walked for brands such as Fendi.

Relationship Status

Giorgia Gabriele is in a romantic relationship with Andrea Grilli. The lovers share a son who was born on July 30, 2020. Moreover, their son’s name is Matteo. Additionally, Andrea is the director of the fashion brand “Marcelo Burlon”.

Before this, she was married to Gianluca Vacchi until 2017. While in a relationship with her, Gianluca Vacchi got himself a tattoo that read ‘Ti amo mia’ on the palm of his hand. It was a sign of his love for her. She and Gianluca met each other in the year 2010 and they began dating three years later. The then-lovers then had their engagement in January 2015.

After her breakup with Gianluca Vacchi, there were rumors that she dated the Italian dancer Stephano de Martino. Moreover, she was also previously romantically involved with footballer Christian Vieri.
