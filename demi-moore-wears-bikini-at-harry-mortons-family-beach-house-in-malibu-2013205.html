Demi Moore Bikini Body: Star, 50, Smiles at Harry Morton Home

Whoa, mama! Demi Moore showed off a healthy glow and fit bikini body ahead of the summer months on Sunday, May 19. The 50-year-old actress sported a red-and-white bikini top and black sweatpants as she hung out at pal Harry Morton's family beach house in Malibu.

Morton, 31, and Moore were spotted hanging out earlier this year, first on a dinner date in Beverly Hills in early January, and then for a late night bite at a West Hollywood club in February (Morton previously dated Lindsay Lohan in 2006.)

But Moore's toned tummy should come as no surprise. The mother of three — to daughters Rumer, 24, Scout, 21, and Tallulah, 19, with ex-husband Bruce Willis — has been spotted frequenting a yoga studio in West Hollywood recently, often with Rumer in tow.

On May 13, the pair were spotted leaving a class together looking chipper and relaxed, with both women wearing oversized shades and cardigans over their workout gear.

The pair were previously estranged after Moore suffered a meltdown in February 2012, and rumors circulated shortly thereafter that Rumer and her younger sisters had filed a restraining order against their mom.
