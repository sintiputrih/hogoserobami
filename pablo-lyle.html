Biography

Pablo Lyle was born in Mazatlan, Mexico on November 18, 1986. Most well known for his portrayal of Cristobal Mendoza Santana on the Mexican telenovela La Sombra del Pasado, he is also notable for his roles in Por Siempre Mi Amor and Cachito de Cielo. He and Maite Perroni were both series regulars on Cachito de Cielo. Before pursuing acting, he worked as a model. He made his television debut in the telenovela Verano de Amor.
