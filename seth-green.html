Biography TimeLine

Seth Green is an American actor, voice actor, comedian, and writer born on February 8, 1974, in Overbrook Park, Philadelphia. He is best known for his work as a writer and performer on the television show “Robot Chicken” and as the voice of Chris Griffin on the hit animated sitcom “Family Guy.”

  • February 8, 1974: Seth Green was born in Overbrook Park, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.
  • 1984: He made his acting debut at 10 in the film “The Hotel New Hampshire.”
  • 1987: Seth landed a starring role in the film “Can’t Buy Me Love,” alongside Patrick Dempsey.
  • 1997 – 2000: He starred as Oz in the popular supernatural drama series “Buffy the Vampire Slayer.”
  • 2001: Seth co-created, wrote, and produced the Adult Swim animated series, “Robot Chicken.”
  • 2005: Seth began voicing Chris Griffin in the animated sitcom “Family Guy.”
  • 2019: Seth Green reprised his role as Oz in the Buffy the Vampire Slayer reboot comic book series.
