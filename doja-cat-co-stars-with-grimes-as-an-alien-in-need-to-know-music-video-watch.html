Doja Cat Co-Stars With Grimes as an Alien in 'Need to Know' Music Video - Watch!

Doja Cat is taking us out of this world.

The 25-year-old “Say So” superstar dropped the music video for her latest song “Need to Know” on Friday (June 11).

Click inside to read more…

In the video, which is set in space, Grimes co-stars as an alien friend to Doja as they enjoy a night out on the town. Grown-ish star Ryan Destiny also appears as an extraterrestrial pal in the clip.

The song is taken from Doja‘s forthcoming third studio album, Planet Her, due out on June 25.

“Wanna know what it’s like / Baby, show me what it’s like / I don’t really got no type / I just wanna f–k all night / Yeah-yeah, oh-woah-woah, baby, I need to know,” she tempts on the track.

Doja also teased the alien theme in a recent performance.
