TNA Posts CM Punk in TNA Wrestling Playlist on YouTube, Banner Stolen from Cleveland During Mo

CM Punk in TNA Wrestling

​TNA has posted a brand new album on YouTube titled "CM Punk in TNA Wresting", which features over a dozen matches from the sort-of-former WWE Superstar. For those that don't know, Punk wrestled briefly for TNA during his early years, when the company had a working deal with indie promotion Ring of Honor. Timing couldn't be better, as CM Punk has been making international headlines for the past 72 hours, and TNA is set to reveal their "mystery investor" – obviously it isn't Punk, but they've done a great job adding to the speculation. 

Banner Stolen from WWE Location

A banner of retired Cavaliers star Austin Carr's jersey was stolen from the Quicken Loans Arena in Cleveland during Monday Night Raw, according to CleveScene.com. The banner was hanging up prior to the WWE event, but was missing in the morning.
