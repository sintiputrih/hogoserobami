Taylor Swift Shares Candid Photos From Her Star-Studded Birthday Bash on Instagram

Forget the Oscars or the Icons Party—it turns out Taylor Swift’s birthday is one of the most star-studded events of the year. The now-34-year-old pop superstar shared a carousel of images to Instagram this afternoon, giving fans an inside look into her cozy birthday bash.

On Wednesday, the “Anti-Hero” singer celebrated with dinner at Freemans, before heading to the Box in the Lower East Side to party with her pals. The A-list attendees included Blake Lively, Zoë Kravitz, Jack Antonoff, Antoni Porowski, Sabrina Carpenter, Gigi Hadid, and Este and Alana Haim.

“Can’t believe this year… actually… happened?” Swift wrote in the caption. “Thank you for all your beautiful birthday wishes yesterday.”

Gotham//Getty Images

For the December 13 event, Swift wore a stretch-mesh black minidress by Clio Peppiatt, embellished with tiny crystals that formed a stunning image of the night sky. Swifties surely understood the look was a reference to her latest studio album, Midnights.

Along with the glittering minidress, the Grammy winner wore a black faux fur jacket from Anine Bing wrapped around her arms, and she rounded out the fit with black velvet platform heels and a silver rhinestone handbag, both from Aquazzura.

As noted by Us Weekly, other famous attendees spotted going to the party included actors Miles Teller and wife Keleigh Sperry. However, one figure was notably absent: Swift’s boyfriend, NFL star Travis Kelce.

Gotham//Getty Images

Kelce sadly had to miss the event because he was at a mandatory practice in Missouri. The tight end and the Kansas City Chiefs will face off against the New England Patriots this Sunday, December 17.

Even without her beau, though, it’s evident TayTay was feeling the love last night.
