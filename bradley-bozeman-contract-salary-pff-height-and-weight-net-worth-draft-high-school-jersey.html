Bradley Bozeman Contract, Salary, PFF, Height And Weight, Net Worth, Draft, High School, Jersey

Bradley Bozeman is an American football center for the Baltimore Ravens of the National Football League. He played college football at Alabama and was drafted by the Ravens in the sixth round of the 2018 NFL Draft.

Bradley Bozeman Contract

Bozeman, 26, was drafted by the Ravens in the sixth round out of Alabama in 2018. He is entering the final year of a four-year, $2.6 million rookie deal.

Bradley Bozeman Salary

Bradley Bozeman salary is $2,183,000, including a $29,602 signing bonus.

Bradley Bozeman PFF

Bozeman’s 81.7 PFF grade is the best of any player on the Ravens roster and ranks second among all centers in the NFL. His 81.7 run-block grade is ranked third at his position while his 73.8 pass-block grade is the second-best among centers.
