Who is Kodak Black dating?

KODAK Black is a popular rapper who first rose to prominence in 2014.

Here’s everything we know about his dating life.

Who is Kodak Black dating?

Kodak Black’s relationship history remains quite a mystery to fans and the public.

Earlier on in the year, Kodak was caught reportedly having sex at a Panthers v. Canucks game in January 2022 but claims he was merely receiving a lap dance from ex-fiance, Mellow Rackz, as he watched the game.

In the past, he has also been reportedly linked to Jayda Cheaves and Cuban Doll.

While nothing has been confirmed, Kodak Black seems to be over the whole relationship game.

In a July 2022 Instagram post, the rapper posted a picture of a quote that said: “I never gained nothing from no relationship I was in – always people taking away from me-” which he captioned, “Never Experienced True Love.”

Later on that week he posted a picture of himself and one of his children, captioned: “I Been Multitasking, Rapping & Being a Daddy To My Lil Children.”

Kodak Black is the father of two children – son, King, and his daughter, Yuri – from two separate women.

King Khalid was conceived with Jammiah Broomfield, while Yuri was conceived with real estate agent, Maranda Johnson, according to TMZ.

In February 2022, however, it was reported that Kodak was expecting his third child with, Daijanae Ward, Complex reported.

“Wore blue and pink to be neutral about everything even tho I wanted a boy kuz I just had a daughter in January I was a lil upset when that pink smoke came out,” he wrote in a since-deleted social media post.

“But it is what it is I know we’ll produce a beautiful healthy baby !!! To be honest I was suppose to have two daughters anyways for my legacy !!!”

Ward often posts pictures with her belly in full view on her Instagram account and has hinted at the baby’s name being “Izzy.”

Who is Kodak Black?

Bill Kahan Kapri, better known as Kodak Black, was born on June 11, 1997.

Kodak is a rapper who first rose to fame in 2014.

In 2017, he released his debut album, which peaked at number three on the Billboard 200 chart.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Kodak Black has an estimated net worth of around $600,000.

The Florida native has a total of three albums: Painting Pictures, Dying to Live, and Bill Israel.

Why was he arrested?

The rapper was reportedly arrested after he was allegedly found in possession of 31 Oxycodone pills and $74,960 in cash, Florida Highway Patrol confirmed to The US Sun.

Cops conducted a traffic stop in the afternoon of July 15, 2022, after Kodak’s vehicle appeared to have windows tinted darker than the legal limit.

Troopers said they saw that the vehicle’s registration had expired – and later discovered his license had, too.

After reportedly smelling marijuana coming from the vehicle, troopers conducted a search and discovered the pills and cash, Highway Patrol told The US Sun.
