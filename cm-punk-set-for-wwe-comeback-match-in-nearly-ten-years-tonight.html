CM Punk Set for WWE Comeback Match in Nearly Ten Years Tonight

WWE is back in action after giving its talent and staff a well-deserved break for Christmas. They’re hitting the road with their Holiday Tour, which features a series of house shows running through this coming Monday night.

One of the most anticipated moments of this tour is the return of CM Punk to WWE. This marks Punk’s first WWE match since the Royal Rumble in 2014, a significant event in wrestling history when he left the company and was subsequently fired on his wedding day. Since then, Punk has been absent from in-ring competition, with his last match occurring at AEW All In, where he triumphed over Samoa Joe in the opening match.

The first matchup fans can look forward to is CM Punk taking on Dominik Mysterio. This showdown will kick off at WWE’s Holiday Tour house show at Madison Square Garden in the heart of New York City tonight. The anticipation is so high that these two will face off again at The Kia Forum in Inglewood, California, on Saturday, December 30.

Here’s a glance at the card for tonight’s MSG house show:

  • WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins defending his title against Drew McIntyre.
  • The long-awaited return of CM Punk as he takes on Dominik Mysterio.
  • A Bullrope Match between Cody Rhodes and Shinsuke Nakamura.
  • Undisputed WWE Tag Team Champions Finn Balor & Damian Priest will be in action against the formidable duo of Sami Zayn & Jey Uso.

The WWE Holiday Tour promises thrilling action and memorable moments, making it a must-see for wrestling enthusiasts during this festive season.
