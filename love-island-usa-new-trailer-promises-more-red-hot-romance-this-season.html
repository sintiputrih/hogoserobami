Love Island USA new trailer promises more red-hot romance this season

Fans of Love Island USA rejoice! Peacock has finally dropped the new trailer and this time the forthcoming fifth season has given a glimpse of what life’s going to be like in the tropical land of Fiji amidst new exciting adventures, twists, and romances.

Recommended Videos

Staying true to the series’ new format, the upcoming entry will feature a group of singles in a faraway secluded villa where they will be subjected to countless challenges, sabotages, and fights to find love. Of course, the ultimate decision will rest on the hands of the audience, who will bear the power to vote out the Islanders they will deem unfit in this race for romance.

The trailer is promising, and expectedly creating quite a buzz. In fact, on the trailer’s basis alone, one can anticipate loads of fun and games are in store for the viewers and fans to root for. New episodes are going to release six days a week, with each of them devoted to new drama, romance, breakups, and recoupling. Adding to the fans’ delight, the release date has also been announced in addition to the trailer, which falls on July 18.

However, this is the second time Fiji is chosen as a perfect exotic location for filming after season one, which was shot in Villa Takali. Las Vegas, Hawaii, and Santa Barbara served as lucrative locations for seasons two, three, and four, respectively.

The wonderful spots accompanied by a great ensemble cast (which has been the norm to date) and intriguing drama have always been the defining features of the series up till now, and it wouldn’t be unreasonable to harbor a similar expectation for season five.

Love Island USA will be streaming on Peacock from July 18.
