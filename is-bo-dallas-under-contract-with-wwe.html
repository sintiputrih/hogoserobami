Bo Dallas: Background in WWE

Bo Dallas, whose real name is Taylor Michael Rotunda, comes from a prestigious wrestling family. He is the son of Mike Rotunda and the grandson of Blackjack Mulligan.

He began his wrestling journey in WWE's developmental system, FCW (Florida Championship Wrestling), before transitioning to NXT and eventually the main roster. During his time in WWE, Bo Dallas achieved success by winning the NXT Championship and being a part of various storylines and characters.

Contractual Status of Bo Dallas with WWE
