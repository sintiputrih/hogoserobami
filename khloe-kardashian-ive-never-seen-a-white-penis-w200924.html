Khloe Kardashian: Ive Never Seen a White Penis

Do you believe this? Khloé Kardashian claimed she’s “never seen a white penis” in a new episode of her late-night FYE talk show Kocktails with Khloé on Wednesday, March 30.

Guest Pauly Shore brought up the TMI conversation by asking Kardashian, 31, why she and her sisters only seem to date black men. At first she tried to brush off the question by calling it “racist” and bringing up the fact that her sister Kourtney Kardashian has three kids with Scott Disick, but the Encino Man actor wouldn’t let it go. He mentioned that Kim Kardashian is married to Kanye West, Kylie Jenner is dating Tyga and Khloé was previously married to Lamar Odom and recently dated James Harden.

The Strong Looks Better Naked author revealed she dated a Moroccan guy before Harden, referring to rapper French Montana — who Shore, 48, argued is “basically” black.

Khloé emphasized that she can only speak for herself, not her family, “I’m not their vagina so all I can speak about is my vagina. It’s all I know though. I’ve never seen a white penis so if you want to show me yours I would like to see it. I’ve never seen one though,” she said. “The relationships that I’ve had tend to be with black men.”

During the episode, the Kocktails With Khloé host also squashed rumors that she’s feuding with her brother Rob Kardashian’s girlfriend, Blac Chyna. “It’s like a soap opera episode, so it sounds very dramatic, which it is,” Khloé said. “But I actually haven’t seen Chyna in a few years, but her and I did not get into a fight, but I do love a good fight.”
