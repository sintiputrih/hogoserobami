Emerald Lodge Turmi, Arba Minch, Ethiopia

On behalf of I would like to take a moment to thank Emerald Resort for the wonderful service that had been provided to NRC staff during our retreat period from 11-14 December 2018.
The services from reception, restaurant and room services were fantastic.
More over the resort team had accompanied us during the visit time to Lake Chamo, Dorze and the Forest to Forty Springs which made our stay successful and enjoyable.
We will also remember the traditional welcome which warms our hearts with joy.
Special thanks to Mr. Getachew, Mr. Ashenafi and the entire squad in Arbaminch who are working at your resort.
