10 Times Beyonc &amp; Jay-Z Were Relationship Goals

When it comes to celebrity relationships, there is no better power couple than Beyoncé and Jay-Z. After being married for twelve years, having three children, and overcoming infidelity, this celeb couple has weathered the storm and can easily be considered relationship goals.

The couple has a combined net worth of about $1.16 billion, according to Business Insider, and their commitment to one another is inspiring. Bey and Jay have seen the world together, performed on stage with a chemistry unmatched, and showed us that they have managed to put their love on top of everything, becoming Hollywood's most envious and powerful married couple. Here are ten times Beyoncé and Jay-Z were relationship goals!

10 A Couple's Vacation To Iceland

In December 2014, Beyoncé and Jay-Z took an amazing couple's trip to Iceland to celebrate the rapper's 45th birthday and it was pure magic. The couple looked like they had a blast, riding around on snowmobiles, wearing adorable matching outfits, and even making angels in the snow. Beyoncé and Jay-Z stayed at the Trophy Lodge, which deems itself a "private luxury resort and spa." This ultimate getaway for the married couple looked perfect and they appeared to be having the time of their lives.

9 Halloween Costumes

When Halloween rolls around, we all wait around to see what couple costume Beyoncé and Jay-Z will come up with next. Their past couple costume's included Barbie and Ken, Team USA gold medalists, and Mexican artists Frida Kahlo and 80s artist Jean-Michel Basquiat.

After having daughter Blue Ivy, the couple has even included her in a few of their Halloween costumes, making them the ultimate celeb family.

8 Sporting Events

Beyoncé and Jay-Z are regulars at NBA games, sitting court side at Brooklyn Nets games, where the rapper was a former co-owner. However, nothing beats this 2012 photo of the couple giving each other a fist bump during a Knicks and Nets game. They always seem to enjoy one another's company as they cheer on their favorite team and have brought Blue Ivy to multiple games with them.

7 2014 Grammy Performance

Beyoncé and Jay-Z have been making music together since 2002 when they dropped "03 Bonnie & Clyde." Since then, they've created countless songs together, music videos, and their performances are some of the most exciting to watch.

One performance, in particular, was during the 2014 Grammy's where Beyoncé and Jay-Z took the stage to perform their hit "Drunk in Love." The performance was steamy and showed the couple's unbreakable bond.

6 Same Mannerisms

Couples who have been together for quite some time can pick up each other's mannerisms, whether it's reciting the same phrases or making the same faces. Fans love when Beyoncé recreates Jay-Z's infamous "mean mug," especially when she and Jay posed together holding up their Grammy awards and mean-mugging together. This is definitely couple goals.

5 Special Moments As A Family

Fans adore the Carter family because they have been through it all. When Beyoncé was given the Michael Jackson Video Vanguard Award at the MTV Video Music Awards in 2014, it was presented by her hubby Jay-Z and daughter, Blue Ivy. It was such a touching moment for the entire family to share together and even rumors of an impending divorce seemed to disappear.

4 Music Videos

Beyoncé and Jay-Z have a long history in partaking in one another's music videos. Starting with 2002's "03 Bonnie & Clyde," the power duo has been in about eight music videos together. Some favorite videos starring the A-list couple include, "Crazy in Love," "Upgrade U," and "Drunk in Love." In 2018, Bey and Jay gave fans what they were always wishing for, and that was producing an album together, titled Everything is Love.

3 All Eyes On Bey

Beyoncé really showed how love conquers all after sharing this special moment with her husband on Instagram. With phone in hand, Jay-Z captures his wife's reactions as they celebrate her birthday. She wrote a lengthy caption, mentioning motherhood, her ten years of marriage with Jay-Z, and touring with the entire family. Her birthday became a moment to not only celebrate herself, but her relationships with others as well.

2 Overcoming Hardships

Jay-Z and Beyoncé's marriage looked shaky when rumors emerged of the rapper's infidelity, and then we all witnessed the video of Beyoncé's sister, Solange Knowles, attacking him in an elevator. Beyoncé even hinted at her husband's cheating in her album "Lemonade." However, it appeared that Jay-Z and Beyoncé reconciled and are better than ever by the endless amounts of photos they post together and as a family.

1 Vow Renewals

Beyoncé and Jay-Z celebrated their ten year wedding anniversary in 2018 and can we just take a minute to appreciate just how awesome this duo is and this photo? The Carter family is relationship goals and one of the coolest couples in Hollywood. Let's all celebrate this celebrity power couple for lasting longer than most celeb marriages.
