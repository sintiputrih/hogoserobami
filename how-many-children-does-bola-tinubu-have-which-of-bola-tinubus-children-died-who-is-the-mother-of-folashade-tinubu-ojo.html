Bola Tinubu is a famous Nigerian accountant and politician who served as the Governor of Lagos State from 1999 to 2007 and Senator for Lagos West during the brief Third Republic. In June 2022, he was chosen as the All Progressives Congress nominee in the 2023 Nigerian presidential election.

He was born to his parents on March 29, 1952, in Lagos, Nigeria, hence he is 70 years. In this article, we will dive into his parental life to know more about it.

How many children does Bola Tinubu have?

Bola Tinubu, accorsing to the information gathered has four children, two boys and two girls. His children are Folashade Tinubu-Ojo, Seyi Tinubu, Jide Tinubu and Abibat Tinubu.

Folashade Tinubu-Ojo and Abibat Tinubu are two beautiful daughters of Bola while Seyi Tinubu and Jide Tinubu are his sons.

Which of Bola Tinubu’s children died?

According to the information gathered, one of the four children of the politician is no more. It appears that one of his sons who has been identified as Jide Tinubu is the one.

He passed away on October 31, 2017, in London, United Kingdom from a heart attack.

Who is the mother of Folashade Tinubu Ojo?

Folashade Tinubu-Ojo is one of the children of Bola. There is little information about her since she has decided to live a private life, however, she happens to be the daughter of Oluremi Tinubu, the current senator of the Lagos Central senatorial district.
