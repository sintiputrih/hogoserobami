Carl Steven Fan Casting

Carlo Steven Krakoff (November 7, 1972 – July 31, 2015), professionally known as Carl Steven, was an Icelandic-American actor, musician and singer. He is best known for his roles in Out of This World and Weird Science. He played as a young Spock in Star Trek III: The Search for Spock (1984). He also voiced Fred Jones in A Pup Named Scooby-Doo.

Steven was diagnosed with pancreatic cancer in December 2013.
