No autumn wardrobe is complete without a cozy sweater, and this Efan pullover is a quintessential pick. I plan to elevate the piece with jeans, skirts, and boots all season — plus, it’ll pair well with comfy leggings and slippers for everyday errands, too. Forget falling leaves and pumpkin spice lattes, Uggs are the real marker of fall in my book. That’s why I’ve already snagged a pair of the Tasman slippers to start the season on the right foot (pun intended). While I grabbed the classic chestnut shade, the pair is available in 12 additional colorways. If you’re still on the fence, take it from one reviewer who said they feel “like walking on little clouds.” 

Gayhay High-Waisted Leggings
