What Are Squishmallows?

At its core, Squishmallows are plush toys. However, their iconic round shape and super soft, plush, and squish-able materials make these plushies stand above the rest. They come in multiple sizes, ranging from extra small to jumbo (which are seriously jumbo!). While they have their own cast of original characters, the brand has so many collabs with popular franchises like Disney, Sanrio, Harry Potter, Peanuts, and Pokémon, which make them a must-have item for collectors.
