Taylor Swift Worries Her Schedule Will Hurt Romance With Travis Kelce As She Flies Across The Countr

Highlights

  • Taylor Swift is making an effort to support Travis Kelce by traveling to his NFL games, but their busy schedules may pose a challenge for their relationship.
  • Taylor celebrated Travis' birthday by traveling from Nashville to Kansas City, but they opted for a low-key evening to avoid attention.
  • Taylor chose not to attend a recent NFL game in order to prepare for the premiere of her new movie, showing her commitment to both her career and her relationship with Travis.

Taylor Swift seems to be head-over-heels for Travis Kelce, and while she seems to have no problem jet setting across the U.S. to support him at his NFL games, it appears the singer may be having concerns that their buy schedules could hinder their romance in the long-run.

Last weekend, Taylor had a busy few days as she went the extra mile to celebrate Travis’ 34th birthday – literally. She made a journey from Nashville to Kansas City on Friday, October 6, to see him a day ahead of his birthday.

On his actual birthday, sources say the new couple chose to have a low-key evening, likely to steer clear of the attention surrounding their romance.

Nevertheless, as Travis and his fellow Kansas City Chiefs teammates traveled to Minnesota for their Sunday, October 8 game, it seemed that Taylor didn’t join, rather opting to return home. NFL commentators officially confirmed on Sunday that the singer was absent from U.S. Bank Stadium in Minneapolis while the Chiefs faced off against the Vikings, despite being present for his last few games before that.

However, there’s likely a good reason Taylor skipped the game – she was preparing for the world premiere of her new movie Taylor Swift: the Eras Tour, which was held in Los Angeles at the Grove on October 11. Travis was noticeably missing from the premiere, as reports suggested he remained in Kansas to get ready for his Thursday game.

Taylor Has To Travel A Lot To Make Her Relationship With Travis Work

If one thing is clear, it’s that Taylor is doing a lot of traveling to make her new romance with Travis work, which one report suggests is becoming a concern for the pop star. Speaking to Us Weekly, a source said both Travis and Taylor are understanding of each other’s work commitments. But while things are going good now, the insider seemed to imply it could become an issue in the future.

“It’s still so new, but they really like each other,” they explained. “They both want to try to see each other when they can and they’re both understanding of each other’s schedules.”

“They’re supportive of each other and they don’t have to be by each other’s side all the time and that feels comfortable,” they went on, saying the pair will split time between Kansas City and New York.“They plan to carve out moments to spend together to get to know each other better.”

Taylor Worries About Being A Distraction At Travis' NFL Games

The insider went on to say that while Taylor wants too attend more of Travis’ games in the future, she doesn’t plan to be there for all. And tha’t snot just because of how difficult it can be traveling to each game given her schedule. Taylor is also worried about being a distraction, given the media attention her appearances bring.

“Taylor doesn’t want to be a distraction to Travis,” they revealed. “She knows it’s not easy when she shows up to his games. It’s a spectacle. She wants to support him and strike a balance while, at the same time, not be distracting to the team.”

While busy schedules and media attention may become an issue in the future, there’s no denying Taylor seems committed to making things work in the interim.
