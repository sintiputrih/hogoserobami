Charles Barkley's Nike Air Max CB 94 Surfaces In &quot;Light Iron Ore&quot;

Charles Barkley is one of the most beloved members of the NBA On TNT crew. Although he sometimes says things that are disagreeable, he is certainly very funny. Overall, fans love tuning in to see what he is going to say next. Sure, Kevin Durant, might hate him, but who actually cares? Barkley's role is to give analysis, be entertaining, and poke the bear. At the end of the day, that is exactly what he does, and it is very hard to hate on him for it. Besides, the man is a legend for crying out loud.

Additionally, Charles Barkley has been in the news a bit more as of late thanks to a shoe he wore in the 90s. Of course, we are talking about the Nike Air Max CB 94. This is a shoe that came out in, you guessed it, 1994. Although it hasn't gotten a ton of shine over the years, it is now making a huge comeback. Overall, fans have enjoyed these new colorways, as evidenced by the numbers they are doing here at HNHH. A couple of colorways have been shown off, including this new "Light Iron Ore" model, pictured below.

More Charles Barkley Sneakers

Image via Nike

In the official images both above and below, you can see how this Charles Barkley signature matches his build and personality. At the end of the day, these embody the 90s Nike Basketball aesthetics. As far as the colorway is concerned, it is very clean. Firstly, we get grey all throughout the upper. Secondly, black is heavily placed on the midsole, tongue, and the straps. Lastly, we get nice pops of orange all the way throughout the shoe.

As far as release dates are concerned, nothing has been announced as of yet. However, these are expected to drop for a price of $170 USD. If you like shoes that have a ton of character within them, these are a must-cop. Let us know what you think of the sneaker, in the comments section down below.
