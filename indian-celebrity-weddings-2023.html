Here Are All The Indian Celebrity Weddings We Witnessed In 2023

Special Mention: Radhika Merchant and Anant Ambani

While this couple didn’t get married, the youngest Ambani heir has officially put a ring on it. The youngest of Mukesh Ambani’s children, Anant, got engaged to his childhood sweetheart Radhika, the daughter of Shaila and Viren Merchant, the billionaire CEO of Encore Healthcare. The couple first had a religious roka ceremony at the Srinathji Temple in Nathdwara on December 29, 2023. Less than a month later, the festivities of their engagement were kicked off with an intimate mehendi. And two days later, the ring ceremony took place at their extravagant Mumbai residence, Antilla, and the occasion saw everybody who’s anybody in attendance—from Sachin Tendulkar to Deepika Padukone, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and even everyone’s favourite Orry! For both of these functions, the to-be-bride wore an exquisite creations by ace designers Abu Jani and Sandeep Khosla.
