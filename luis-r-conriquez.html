Overall Meaning

The song "El Buho" by Luis R Conriquez is about the struggles of life in Tijuana, a Mexican border city. The song opens with the artists introducing themselves and their upcoming corrido, "El Buho," which they are ready to perform. The lyrics then go on to describe the violence and conflicts in Tijuana, with the streets resembling hell due to constant turmoil. The artist then goes on to describe himself as someone who is focused on his work and not interested in fighting, but if someone messes with him, he can defend himself by dealing with the gringos and keeping his clients happy.


The lyrics express the artist's tough experiences in life, including having suffered from hunger and choosing the streets over school. He is proud of the fact that he has lived through all of it and is not afraid of getting involved in trouble. He then goes on to identify himself as 'El Buho de Tijuana', someone who is observant and takes care of their territory.


The song tells the story of a person who has lived through tough times but has managed to rise above it all. The song is a representation of the struggles that people face and overcoming them to become successful. The lyrics capture the spirit of resilience and hard work, inspiring those who listen to it.
