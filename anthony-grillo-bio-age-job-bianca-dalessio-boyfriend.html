Who Is Selling The Hampton Bianca DAlessio Boyfriend, Anthony Grillo?

Anthony Grillo is the boyfriend of Bianca D’Alessio, who is one of the new cast members of the Discovery Plus real estate reality show, Selling the Hamptons. And in this article, we tell you everything you need to know about Anthony Grillo.

Let’s start with Selling the Hamptons. Selling the Hamptons is now available on Discovery Plus for streaming. And apparently, it is a very different show from all the other real estate reality shows on TV right now. The Hamptons real estate scene is very different from everywhere else and it is very difficult to find success for new realtors there.

And Selling the Hamptons is going to bring that side of the reality to the screen as well. Apart from Bianca, the show will also feature J.B. Andreassi, Michael Fulfree, Peggy Zabakolas, Jimmy Giugliano, Kenny Arias, Mia Calabrese, and a few others as the cast members.

Meet Selling The Hampton Bianca D’Alessio Boyfriend, Anthony Grillo

Anthony Grillo and Bianca D’Alessio first met in 2015. Since then they have been together through thick and thin. They have had many enjoyable times, from “fancy dinners” to trips to Europe, to just being a darling couple on social media.

And he does not mind her working late or on weekends and even listens when she talks about real estate all the time. According to Bianca, Anthony also makes her laugh all the time and keeps a smile on her face.

Similarly, Anthony has called Bianca an “amazing woman”, “a beautiful person in every way”, his “pea head”, “ultimate traveler”, “Lil fisherwoman”, and other names of endearment. He also said in an Instagram post that Bianca has “balanced [him] in every way”.

Furthermore, Anthony has also mentioned that Bianca deals with his “craziness” quite well. Also, the two of them travel a lot.

It is unclear whether Bianca and Anthony have tied the knot. For two people who are both quite active on social media not sharing anything about their wedding can only mean that they have not married yet. Although in 2016, they did go to a wedding as each other’s date.

However, Bianca has not deleted the old photos of her and Anthony on her IG. So, it was likely that they were not broken up.

Anthony Grillo Job

Anthony Grillo has many jobs, but all the jobs he is associated with are related to coins and metals. It is safe to say that Anthony is a coin expert. According to his Instagram bio, Anthony is the president of Westchester County Coin Club. He was elected as the president of the club in 2019 and he has held the position since.

On the occasion of his election, Anthony posted a photo with Joel Iskowitz, a legendary American Mint Engraver, and wrote, “Being elected President of the Westchester County Coin Club of New York has been a Great Honor. We had our annual Banquet on Wednesday. I invited Joel Iskowitz, US Mint Engraver to be our Guest of Honor, He spoke incredibly.”

Anthony is also a senior numismatic buyer for American Collector’s Group. According to their website, American Collector’s Group is a “company that focuses and specializes on the buy-side of the industry. Our highly trained ANA educated buyers to show a true passion for the numismatic and precious metal industry…”.

On the other hand, according to his Linkedin profile, Anthony has been working for the past 12 years as a Senior Sales Account Manager for National Collectors Mind/Stratus Com. He started that job in January 2010 and has been at it since then. It is possible that Anthony has not updated his Linkedin profile in a while.
