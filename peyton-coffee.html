Who is Peyton Coffee?

Peyton Coffee is a young American TikTok star.

The young Tiktoker first started her social media career on Vine. Jason Coffee, her father, was himself quite a sensation on Vine.

Like most Vine stars, Peyton, too, chose to go the TikTok route and created quite an impressive presence on the social networking platform.

Peyton was trolled online for posting a TikTok, asking for more followers. Peyton talked about how she was “so close” to 13 million followers in the video. However, she was, in fact, 700,000 followers away from reaching 13 million followers.

Peyton’s fans sent her gifts for their account names to appear on her wall. However, when Peyton showed herself painting over the wall, many fans expressed their disappointment.

One of the users even accused Peyton of “using her followers for money.” Similarly, another TikTok user demanded a refund.

Likewise, another one of the users described an unpleasant experience that he had with Peyton. After receiving a gift from him, he stated that Peyton Coffee unfollowed him on Tiktok.

In contrast, many adoring fans of Peyton came to her defense. They explained how fans were acting immaturely and that it was her choice to do whatever she wanted with her “fan wall.”

Many went on to say that Peyton probably painted over the wall since her family had bought a house and was about to move out.

Peyton herself chose not to be entangled in the controversy and did not give a statement to clear the accusations.
