When thinking about Melissa Rauch, the first thing that comes to mind is the sweet and smart Bernadette from The Big Bang Theory. Let's take a closer look at Melissa Rauch's net worth, revealing not just the size of her fortune but also the way she puts her money to good use. From her earnings on The Big Bang Theory to everything else she is involved in, here's how much Melissa Rauch is worth, how she spends her impressive fortune, and what she is doing now.

How Does Melissa Rauch Spend Her Very Impressive Net Worth?

Melissa Rauch keeps her spending habits very private and not much is known about how she spends her impressive net worth. However, one thing many don't know about Rauch is that she's dedicated to giving back through charity, just like many other celebrities. Melissa Rauch invests her money and energy into Oscar's Kids, a nonprofit she co-founded to raise awareness and funds for Diffuse Intrinsic Pontine Glioma (DIPG), a rare pediatric cancer.

Oscar's Kids was created in memory of Oscar Keogh, a young boy who battled DIPG. Oscar was a fan of Melissa Rauch's character on The Big Bang Theory, which sparked a meaningful connection between them. Melissa Rauch revealed her friendship with Oscar's family inspired her to become an advocate for DIPG awareness. She said:

As far as my background, and not having experience running a charity, the only thing I can authentically say is that I had the passion for becoming an advocate for this cause based on one of the most inspirational friendships in my life with Oscar Keogh’s family.

Unfortunately, Oscar passed away at the age of five, highlighting the urgent need for research and support for DIPG patients. Melissa Rauch and her husband oversee the U.S. chapter of Oscar's Kids, focusing on fundraising to support the work of Dr. Michelle Monje at Stanford University, who has been conducting groundbreaking research in the field of pediatric neural oncology​​​​​​.

Melissa Rauch's involvement with Oscar's Kids reflects her deep commitment to making a positive impact. Through events like Oscar's PJ Party, the charity aims to provide hope and assistance to families affected by DIPG. They also plan to collaborate with the Ronald McDonald House in LA to bring comfort to families undergoing treatment. Melissa's dedication to DIPG research demonstrates her desire to use her resources for the greater good.

Melissa Rauch has over the years built a remarkable net worth of $20 million. This very impressive figure stems from her diverse talents in acting, writing, directing, and producing, according to Celebrity Net Worth. She discovered her passion for comedy and acting early on. Despite earning a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree from Marymount Manhattan College in 2002, her journey in the entertainment world began during college as a stand-up comedian.

Where Is Mellisa Rauch Today?

Melissa Rauch is currently dazzling audiences in the reboot of the classic TV show Night Court. In the new version of Night Court, which premiered its second season on January 2, 2024, Melissa Rauch plays the chipper and good-hearted character Abracadabra "Abby" Stone​​​​.

Not only does Rauch grace the screen with her presence, but she also serves as an executive producer, deeply involved in maintaining the essence of the original series while infusing it with fresh energy. Her dedication to the project is evident, as she strives to uphold the spirit of the original while introducing new elements to the sequel.

Throughout Season 2, Rauch's character, Abby Stone, evolves and becomes more adept, reflecting her growth and adjustment to the vibrant backdrop of New York City.

Joining Rauch in the Night Court reboot is John Larroquette, who has expressed admiration for working alongside her, highlighting the strong camaraderie among the cast members. The first season of Night Court aired in 2023, and consisted of 16 episodes, while the second season started airing in December 2023.

Melissa Rauch is happily married to Winston Beigel, whom she met in college. They have two children and have collaborated on numerous writing and production projects, showcasing their strong partnership both personally and professionally.
