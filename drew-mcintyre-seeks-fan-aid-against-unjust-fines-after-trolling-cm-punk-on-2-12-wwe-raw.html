Drew McIntyre Seeks Fan Aid Against Unjust Fines After Trolling CM Punk on 2/12 WWE RAW

Drew McIntyre has made it his life’s mission to go after CM Punk every single week after the latter’s injury. He got fined for trolling Punk on RAW and has now urged fans to help him pay the unjust fines levied on him.

During this week’s episode of Monday Night RAW, Adam Pearce confronted Drew McIntyre backstage on the show after catching him wearing a T-shirt mocking CM Punk, which he introduced last week on Monday Night RAW.

Drew McIntyre apologized to Pearce and removed the T-shirt, revealing another inside it. As The Scottish Warrior was leaving, Pearce yelled at him, making it known that his fine had been tripled.

Drew McIntyre took to Twitter and commented on a video of himself getting fined by Adam Pearce, as he urged fans to buy his merchandise to pay off the unjust fines.

“Buy my shirt to help me pay these unjust fines #WWERAW”

Drew McIntyre is set to square off against Cody Rhodes on next week’s episode of Monday Night RAW, so we will have to wait and how The Scottish Warrior will fare against The American Nightmare.
