GT Dave Wiki (Kombucha) Age, Net worth, Bio, Wife, Family &amp; Facts

GT Dave (born in 1978) is an American Entrepreneur, Founder, and Chief Executive Officer from Los Angeles, California. He is famous for being the owner of a company titled “GT’s Living Food“. Furthermore, he is popularly known as “The Kombucha Billionaire” in the Media.

Furthermore, it is a firm that deals in handcrafts small batches of raw and organic Kombucha Tea. Dave founded at the age of 17 in 1995. Moreover, GT’s Living Foods produces GT’s Kombucha, CocoKefir, CocoYo, Synergy, Alive, and VeggieKefir.

This venture is family owned and operated since 1995. Kombucha is a fermented probiotic beverage that GT Dave turned into a $600 million USD category. They started to sell the product in local health-food markets.

Later, Kombucha went national thanks to Whole Foods and currently present at Safeway and even Walmart. According to a source, GT never spent his money on the advertisement for the product.

He wanted that the product should speak for itself. Then, due to its various benefits, many big celebrities like Madonna, Gwyneth Paltrow, Reese Witherspoon, etc began to drink it.

Career, Lifestyle & Success Story

The 39-year-old GT Dave started his career in 1995 as an entrepreneur. His family used to suffer from various health issues in the past. His parents later started to consume homebrewed kombucha which played a very important role in their life.

In July 1994, Dave’s mom, Laraine found a lump in her breast. Furthermore, doctors diagnosed with fast-growing cancer. The cancer was 3 inches and she only had 1 year to live. Fortunately, Laraine drank Kombucha every day.

Then, doctors became shocked after the lumpectomy, they found that her disease was healing rapidly. Furthermore, GT eventually decided to spread this miracle drink all over the world.

He was about 17 years old when he began the firm in his parents’ kitchen. Moreover, with little help, the CEO managed to create his own venture. He also rented a 2,000-square-foot industrial space in Gardena, just outside of L.A. It is been 24 years that GT’s Living Food is going strong.
