Taylor Lautner's Net Worth Changed Significantly After He Basically Vanished From Hollywood

Highlights

  • Taylor Lautner's significant hiatus from acting led him to explore alternative income streams outside of Hollywood.
  • Despite his retreat from acting, Lautner made lucrative real estate investments and continues to earn from his Twilight saga profits.
  • With a net worth of $45 million, Lautner's recent acting appearances and potential partnerships hint at a Hollywood return.

Taylor Lautner went from being one of Hollywood's most sought-after young actors to completely disappearing from the limelight in the mid-2010s. A few years after becoming a fan-favorite for his iconic role in the Twilight Saga franchise, the actor went on a lengthy career hiatus. His significant withdrawal from the movie industry impacted his acting career. Considering that most of his earnings came from acting, did Lautner's net worth take a hit?

Several speculations arose about why the movie star took a break from acting, one of which hinted at his mental health. However, being absent from the acting world did not stop Taylor Lautner from exploring other lucrative business ventures to keep his earnings afloat. In this article, we will explore the former child star's net worth after his Hollywood retirement and look into the possibility of his career comeback.

Taylor Lautner's Earnings And Net Worth After His Acting Break

One of Taylor Lautner's last known projects before his lengthy acting break was a recurring role in the comedy horror series Scream Queens. After that, the actor retired to a more private life in California with his wife, Taylor Dome. The couple has since been invested in their joint podcast, The Squeeze, which revolves around topics of mental health.

According to Forbes, there are different ways to make money from podcasts, one of which is through sponsored ads. Running sponsored ads implies that companies pay the podcaster to read scripts that promote existing products. Other ways to profit from owning podcasts include affiliate codes or links, consulting, gated content or memberships, advertising networks, and accepting donations and tips.

Considering Taylor Lautner's already established fanbase and celebrity status, it is safe to say that the podcast is thriving. He is not new to endorsements, having collaborated with brands like American Express, Candies, and Sony Pictures.

The Abduction actor is also into real estate, as he has been in the news a few times for some of his significant purchases and sales. Taylor Lautner has owned several homes over the years, including a $3.8 million fancy Mediterranean house in Agoura Hills, which he purchased in 2020.

Two years later, in April 2022, Lautner put it up for sale for $5.25 million, and star actress Kaley Cuoco became the new owner. The Adventures of Sharkboy And Lavagirl actor's real estate investments date back to his early twenties when he bought his first home in 2012.

The former teen idol bought a luxury 10,000-square-foot property in the San Fernando area of Los Angeles for $1.86 million. This was a great deal for Lautner at the time because the house, initially priced at $2.2 million, was sold off by a financial institution that repossessed it from NFL star Marcellus Wiley. Lautner's great business acumen came into play in 2020 when he sold it for $4.2 million. In addition to this, the actor still owns a 10-acre property in Castaic, California, which he purchased in 2011 for $2 million.

Despite his acting hiatus, the Cheaper by the Dozen actor still has a stake in the movie industry. The star is entitled to 2.5% of all the profits from the Twilight Saga series. From 2008 to 2012, Lautner played the iconic role of the teen shapeshifter Jacob Black, which earned him critical acclaim. The Teen Choice Award winner made over $12 million each for every Twilight Saga installation he appeared in. According to CA Knowledge, Lautner makes about $4 million yearly. So, what is Taylor Lautner's net worth? The Twilight star is worth $45 million, largely earned from his acting career and investments.

Will Taylor Lautner Make A Hollywood Comeback?

In 2022, fans saw Taylor Lautner return to his day job when he appeared in the Adam Sandler-produced family comedy Home Team. It is safe to say that the actor came out of retirement to work with Sandler, who is his longtime collaborator. The pair started working together in 2013 after Lautner did a cameo on Grown Ups 2. He appeared in more of the comedian's film projects like The Ridiculous 6.

Once speaking about working with Sandler's company, Happy Maidson, the TV actor relayed that it felt like "summer camp," and he would "say yes to a Happy Madison project for the rest of my life." It is safe to say that fans might see more of Taylor Lautner in movies if Adam Sandler decides to continue casting him in future projects. However, as of 2023, Lautner's IMDb profile did not list any upcoming projects.
