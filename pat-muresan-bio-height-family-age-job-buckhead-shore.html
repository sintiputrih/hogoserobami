Who Is Pat Muresan? His Bio, Height, Family, Age, Job

Pat Muresan wasn’t on Buckhead Shore for trouble. The charming guy joined the popular MTV franchise to spread love. So, it’s no surprise that whenever any of the cast was in trouble, especially his friend Parker Lipman, Pat rushed to their aid and got them out of any predicament.

Get to know this savior better as this Pat Muresan Bio proceeds. 

Pat Muresan On Buckhead Shore

Joining the Shore franchise, MTV’s brand new reality series Buckhead Shore premiered on June 23, 2022, and just like the original, it saw 9 longtime friends chronicling “Lake Life” in Atlanta’s upscale Buckhead, Georgia. Yes, lake life! The “Shore” part of the title doesn’t actually refer to an ocean. It was just put there to let the viewers know that this was just another show in the Jersey Shore franchise.

“Are y’all ready to wild the BUCK out? 🤯 Don’t miss the SERIES PREMIERE of #BuckheadShore Thursday, June 23rd at 9/8c on @MTV,” Pat shared his excitement for the premiere v.i.a IG.

According to the star, the cast on Buckhead Shore respected each and loved each other as a group. “Parker is a brother to me and Adamo and Katie — the whole group. I don’t think we have that choice of splitting up because if Parker and I were in a fight today, somebody in the group is going to fix it and get us to talk it out. I don’t think we were worried about that. We’re family,” he explained.

And Simmons agreed to him. “We’re family, and we know each other. We’re not put together, we grew up together, some of us, but we all are friends,” he added.

Joining Pat for this inaugural season were his co-stars — JuJu Barney, Katie Canham, Savannah Gabriel, Parker Lipman, Bethania Locke, Adamo Giraldo, Chelsea Prescott, and DJ Simmons.

Is Pat Muresan Married?

No, Pat Muresan wasn’t married when he appeared on Buckhead Shore. However, he was about to!

This charmer got engaged to the make-up artist of Formal Faces, Laurel Kate Anderson on New Year 2022. Wondering about the ring? Well, it was big enough — a sophisticated pear-shaped handcrafted diamond right!

Looking back, Pat and Laurel were an item at least before 2018. Fast forward to November 2021, Pat then revealed the good news about their pregnancy through an IG post captioned, “as if God hasn’t blessed me enough throughout my life… it’s official, the love of my life and I are expecting our biggest blessing! April 2022 can’t come soon enough.”

On April 25, 2022, his firstborn (7 lbs 2 ounces and 21 inches) Crew Beckham Muresan finally stepped into this world. And again, Pat took it to his IG to share the good news and mentioned that his son was the “most beautiful angel.”

Also, he added that they had a long labor. But thankful, Crew was born “health and so beautiful.”

Talking a little about Pat’s finance, Laurel loves her job as a make-up artist. “I get to accentuate inner and outer beauty through art/cosmetics on the best day of someone’s life,” she explained. Her favorite makeup products were Tarte Shape Tape, YSL All Hours Foundation, and Mac Creme D Nude lipstick.

Besides makeup, Laurel was also a voice-over artist.

Overall, Laurel was Pat’s “rock, better half,  biggest blessing,  queen, forever, and EVERYTHING.”

On Laurel’s birthday in 2018 (i.e on November 6), Pat captioned an IG post, “happy birthday to the most amazing, most beautiful, and most caring person I know. I am beyond blessed to have you in my life. Can’t wait to celebrate with you today! I love you to the moon and back.”
