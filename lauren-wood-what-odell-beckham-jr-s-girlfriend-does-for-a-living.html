What Odell Beckham Jr.'s Girlfriend Does For A Living

Lauren Wood works as an Instagram personality, model, and actor, and most notably, she worked as a model on Nick Cannon's MTV hit Wild 'n Out. With over 1 million followers on Instagram, Wood often posts photos modeling and promoting different products and brands. 

Additionally, the fitness junkie regularly shares photos and videos of her workout regimen. In September 2020, she posted a snap of herself taking a short study break, although it's not clear what she's hitting the books for. "Took a lil break but back at it. Self study is boring," she captioned the pic. "I'm a hands on learner, but I'm going to finish this so I can start making plans & actually training." No matter what Wood is doing, she doesn't take her success for granted. "I worked a 9 to 5 job for so long and then I would have never imagined now I'd be like, 'OK, I'm going to cut a check off of an Instagram post,'" she told FabTV in 2018 about her influencer career.

It's pretty clear Wood is an independent woman and working to secure her bag — no wonder why Beckham Jr. is head over heels!
