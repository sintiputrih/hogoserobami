Taylor Blake - Age, Family, Bio

Social media personality who is particularly known for her hiitaylorblake Instagram page. She has gained popularity there for her frequent posts detailing her everyday life. She is an animal lover, and frequently posts with her animals on her Instagram account. She runs the threeroaddogs Instagram account where she shares photos of her dogs.

Before Fame

She published her first post to Instagram on October 5, 2013.

Trivia

She has amassed more than 240,000 followers to her Instagram page. Her spoken word poem, "Boys Will Be Boys," which was published to her self-titled YouTube channel, has earned more than 10,000 total views. She has been represent by MGMT Society management label.

Family Life

She has two sisters. She has been in a relationship with fellow Instagram star Kristian Haggerty.

Associated With

She and fellow Instagrammer Adrianne Ho are both known for their personal and stylistic photos.
