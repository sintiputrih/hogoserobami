AEW International Title Match Set For 5/5 AEW Dynamite

The May 10 episode of AEW Dynamite will feature an AEW International Championship match.

As confirmed during the May 3 episode, Orange Cassidy will defend the AEW International Championship against Daniel Garcia of the Jericho Appreciation Society on Wednesday. Cassidy and Garcia both competed in the eight-man tag team bout where “Freshly Squeezed”, Adam Cole, Bandido, and Roderick Strong beat the Jericho Appreciation Society on Wednesday.

As previously announced, Kenny Omega and Jon Moxley will face off in a Steel Cage Match. Omega issued the challenge after the Blackpool Combat Club attacked The Elite and Konosuke Takeshita on the April 26 episode of AEW Dynamite, and Moxley accepted. Moxley and Omega both cut heated promos about the match on the May 3 episode of AEW Dynamite.

Plus, AEW World Tag Team Champions FTR (Dax Harwood and Cash Wheeler) will speak on the show. Harwood ad Wheeler are coming off of singles losses to Jeff Jarrett and Jay Lethal. On the May 3 episode, Lethal challenged the duo to a title match at AEW Double or Nothing.

The updated card for the May 10 episode of AEW Dynamite is as follows:

  • : Orange Cassidy (c) vs. Daniel Garcia
  • We’ll hear from AEW World Tag Team Champions FTR
  • : Jon Moxley vs. Kenny Omega

WrestleZone will have coverage of AEW Dynamite as it airs on Wednesday.
